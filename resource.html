What factors affect my loan EMI amount in Canada and US?

Several factors influence your loan EMI, including the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure. Higher loan amounts or interest rates increase your EMI, while a longer tenure reduces it. What is the difference between fixed and floating interest rates for US loans?

A fixed interest rate keeps your EMI constant throughout the loan tenure, offering predictable payments for US home loans or personal loans. A floating interest rate varies with market conditions, potentially changing your EMI for US car loans or other loans.
