How to Use the Loan EMI Calculator

Our Loan EMI Calculator helps you estimate your monthly payments for loans in the US and Canada. Simply select your market, enter the loan amount, annual interest rate, and tenure in years. Click "Calculate EMI" to see your estimated monthly payment and an amortization schedule.

US vs. Canada: In the US, interest is typically compounded monthly, while in Canada, mortgage interest is compounded semi-annually. Our calculator adjusts the interest rate accordingly to provide accurate estimates.
